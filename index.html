Who is "Alex Turner"?

Hello! My name is Alex Turner and I'm a graduate of Creative Writing at Bath Spa University. I stumbled into learning how to code initially because I wanted to extend my skillset as a writer and storyteller -- but, lo and behold, I have ended up rather inspired!
I now have a solid foundation in coding and technical skills, with certifications in Python, SQL, HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, and UX/UI design. I have developed websites from scratch (like this one!), worked with WordPress, and created small text-based RPGs featuring inventory and battle systems using Python. I also have experience using Git for version control and have contributed to website development projects at my old job by assisting with content updates, troubleshooting, and enhancing user experience. My ability to quickly learn new technologies allows me to blend creativity with technical expertise in game development and interactive storytelling -- my personal passions.
